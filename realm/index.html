Design

As one third of the design team, I am ultimately responsible for getting website visitors to start to use Realm (the product). I accomplish this by making the website easy to navigate & by ensuring that visitors are aware of what the product is. I've touched almost every part of the website, whether by leading features or by collaborating with other people.

Web development

Part of what makes design at Realm fun is that the designers are also the builders, so we get to own our projects from start to finish. I've had the chance to learn some of the basics of web development, which then inspired me to build the site you're reading this on. (Feel free to leave some notes on the source code.)
